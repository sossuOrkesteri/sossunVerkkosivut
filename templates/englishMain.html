SOSSu is the orchestra of Savo Student Nation of the University of Helsinki. This 12 year old orchestra has over 30 players; strings, brass, woodwinds and rhythm section. Sometimes you can hear also a little bit different kind of instruments played in SOSSu, for example a typewriter!

SOSSu is eager to play whenever it’s possible and we have gigs in wide range of student union and university parties. We do also wedding – and other party gigs. If 30 persons sounds like a too big orchestra, we can also arrange smaller groups to play in YOUR party. SOSSu plays wide selection of dance music from academic dances to pop music. If you’re interested – don’t hesitate to contact us @ sossu.orkesteri@gmail.com!
