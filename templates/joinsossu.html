Join SOSSu!

Would you like to be part of a student orchestra in Helsinki?

Are you an exchange student or non-Finnish speaker student in Helsinki University, Aalto University or some other school in Helsinki area? Do you play some kind of classical instrument? Do you want to be part of a jolly orchestra and learn about Finnish student culture among it? SOSSu might just be the place for you!

Our weekly rehearsals take place on the top floor of New Student house (Mannerheimintie 5 A) on Tuesdays at 6pm to 9pm. Rehearsals are mostly in Finnish, but the language of music is universal and we always try to translate tricky parts to our non-Finnish speakers. We usually hold entrance playing for new players with a small group after our normal weekly rehearsals. If you want to be part of us or ask more about us, please contact us via e-mail sossu.orkesteri@gmail.com!
